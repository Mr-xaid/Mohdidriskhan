Products We Offer

Beds

A bed is an item of furniture that is used as a place to sleep, rest, and relax. Most modern beds consist of a soft, cushioned mattress on a bed frame.

Case Goods

Casegoods, or case furniture, refers to pieces of furniture that are typically constructed from solid wood or wood veneers and have non-upholstered surfaces. Casegoods include tables, hutches, dressers, chests, and other pieces which provide function or storage capabilities.

Tables

A table is an item of furniture with a raised flat top and is supported most commonly by 1 to 4 legs (although some can have more). It is used as a surface for working at, eating from or on which to place things.

Seating

Seating is a broad term used to describe furniture designed to allow people to sit or recline. Most types of seating include a horizontal seat (or seat pan), a form of upright back support and legs or some other support device attached to the underside of the seat pan.

Miscellaneous

A magazine case or a coat stand, a stool, a showcase unit, or a shoe case, you can discover all these are miscellaneous furniture in our collection. Guided by functionality and high aesthetics, we make sure to offer you only the best products for each space!

Inlay

Inlay is commonly used in the production of decorative furniture, where pieces of colored wood, precious metals or even diamonds are inserted into the surface of the carcass using various matrices including clear coats and varnishes.
